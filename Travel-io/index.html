About us

How did we start our company life?

Our company, which was founded in 1925, has been meeting with references and working with them since 1928.

Our first partners

Our first business partner was Turkish Airlines. We have been working together since 1929.

Later, American Airways joined us with the partnership agreement we signed in 1932.

Our partners and us

We provided assistance to our partners in every way and always stood by them no matter what. In this way, we further increased our partners' trust in us.

Our awards

Over the years As our company's partners increased, our developing and growing company began to receive awards. When we received our first award, WORLD AIRLINE 2015, our partner number was 28.

Our company in today's time

As our number of partners increases, we continue our career as a more trusted and recognized airline company and we are proud of serving our partners.
